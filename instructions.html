Instructions for Simple Delicious Meals

Here are instructions for the meals currently listed. More instructions will be added soon as the website is still under maintenance.

THE NUT CRACKER

  1. Place the slice of bread on a plate. (Optional: toast or pan-cook the bread first.)
  2. Spread peanut butter onto the bread using a butter knife or spoon or anything available
  3. EAT MEAL
  4. Go to Recipes page if needed

PIZZA THE LONG WAY

  1. Slice the long bread in half.
  2. Spread pizza sauce onto the bread. (Optional: add sauce last if you prefer.)
  3. Add cheese on top of the sauce.
  4. Add pepperoni slices. (Optional: place under cheese if preferred.)
  5. Sprinkle Italian herb seasoning for flavor.
  6. Cook in oven or toaster oven, or eat as-is.
  7. EAT MEAL

THE SUB

  1. Cook meatballs according to package or recipe instructions.
  2. Place roll on a plate and add the cooked meatballs.
  3. Pour marinara sauce over meatballs.
  4. (Optional) Add cheese on top.
  5. (Optional) Add Italian herb seasoning.
  6. EAT MEAL
