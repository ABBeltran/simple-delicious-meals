Instructions for Simple Delicious meals

Here are some instructions for the currently listed meals when the required recipes are obtained. The list is small because the website is still under maitenance

  • Peanut butter sandwhich

  • 1. Place the slice of bread on a plate of your choice (Optional: Toast bread in a toaster or cook in a pan before placing bread on the plate)
  • 2. Open the jar of Peanut butter and place the cream smoothly onto the bread with a butterknife,spoon,or anything available.
  • 3. EAT MEAL
  • 4. Recipes page if needed

  • Pizza bread

  • 1. Slice long bread in half with a knife
  • 2. Place pizza sauce on bread (Optional: Place sauce last if this is your preferred way)
  • 3. Place cheese on top of the sauce
  • 4. Place peperoni on top of the cheese (Optional:place under the cheese if it is your preferred way)
  • 5. Sprinkle italian herb seasoning on top for more flavor really
  • 6. put meal in oven or toaster oven to cook the meal or EAT MEAL as is idk
  • 7. EAT MEAL

  • Meatball Sandwhich

  • 1. Cook Meatballs on what the instructions tell you to
  • 2. place the roll on plate and then serve the cooked meatballs on roll
  • 3. place marinira sauce on cooked meatballs
  • 4. EAT MEAL or place cheese on top
  • 5. EAT MEAL or place italian herb seasoning on top
  • 6. EAT MEAL
